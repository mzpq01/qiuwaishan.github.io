About Riyadh GO

Riyadh GO is a comprehensive visualization of your real-world travelling cost in terms of Money and Time from Riyadh hotspots. You can easily explore how far can you travel by Uber within 30 minutes and 50 SAD (local currency).
Foursquare checkin data of Riyadh are visualized as node size and heat gradient to define the hotspots. Riyadh transportation OD analysis data and congession simulation data from MIT HuMNet LAB are deployed to visualize the real time cost. Uber cost estimate algorithm is deployed to assess the comprehensive real-world cost of Money, which is a combination of time and distance.
